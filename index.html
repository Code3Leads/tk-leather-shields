About TK Leather Shields

TK Leather Shields and Designs was established in 2009 by Tony, a firefighter, and Karin McGillin, a paramedic. Starting in our garage as a modest hobby has evolved into a booming business.

Over the past 16 years, we have created and shipped products across the US and look forward to continued growth!

We are dedicated to providing exceptional craftsmanship and innovative designs at affordable prices. We welcome new ideas and challenges and are excited to work with you on your custom design.
